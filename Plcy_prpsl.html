Write a Policy Proposal!

But before you begin, follow these steps

Choose the topic:

  • Find a topic that you are passionate on and interested in!
  • Make sure that this topic is a prevalant issue in today's society.

Writing the policy statement

  • DO RESEARCH! Make sure you use viable resources
  • Use statistics and valid points to back up your points
  • Present a specific plan, and make it clear and understandable!

After you're finished

  • If you want to send it to an official, address it to an official who has the most authority over the topic that you selected. Go to the 'Contact your Representative' for more information
  • Save it to the forum!
